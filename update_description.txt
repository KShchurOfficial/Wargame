-!--!--!--!- Update 1.0.1 -!--!--!--!-

1) System of division choosing has improved.
2) Game settings have added. Now user can set size of battlefield and amount of players (from 2 to 4).
3) Prototype of economics has added. Now each player receive 1000 coins on the game start.
These coins player should spend to buy at least one division. Amount of divisions each player can place 
is limited by their coin amount. At the moment player can grab more coins by destroying enemy divisions.
